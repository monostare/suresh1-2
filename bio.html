Jeyram Educational Trust

Introduction

JEYRAM Educational Trust ( JET Skills ) is a registered Trust under the Indian Trust ACT 1882 , a Non – Government Organization ( NGO ) ,
formed with the vision to create a strong platform credible and enabling all the underprivileged category of our society and uplift them
towards earning their livelihoods .

We at JET Skills develop into adding value to our Skill India Vision and thus approach all skill training activities keeping in mind the vulnerable
student’s perspective and their lives first . We as a collective , promote high – quality skill training and employment , firmly believe that the
Skill India initiative flagged by the Government of India will empower the youth of the country with skill sets that will make them more
employable and productive in their work environment .

JET Skills is working in collaboration with NSDC , the SSCs and the State , Central Government schemes has consultative status at the
Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship to push for ethical standards in skilling , to provide feedback on youth interests ,
aspirations on skill programs and to reach out to provide necessary skills to thousands of youths across PAN India .

The Institute’s unique training approach is well designed for delivering the Industry demanding , employment linked suitable skill training
with Speed , Scale , and Standards . JET Skills is one among the most crucial partners for the Government’s flagship scheme of
Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra ( PMKVY ) and DDU – GKY .

Objective of Skill Development Projects

The main objective of JET Skills in to the Education Industry is to provide adequate training in market – relevant skills to many un – employed youths in India . Training at JET Skills ensures holistic development of trainees . In addition to technical skills , training at JET Skills also focuses on other dimensions of skill building , such as – numerical ability , science , basic IT , industry orientation , communication , soft skills & personality development , and work ethics , and places special emphasis on Safety , Health & Environment ( SHE ) considering the sector’s specific need .

JET Skills strongly believe that the future training comprise skills , abilities , and learning dispositions that have been identified as being required for success in future . This is part of a growing international movement focusing on the skills required for students to master in preparation for success in a rapidly changing , digital society . Many of these skills are also associated with deeper learning , which is based on mastering skills such as analytic reasoning , complex problem solving , and teamwork . These skills differ from traditional academic skills in that they are not primarily content knowledge – based .
